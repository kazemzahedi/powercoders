  1. What does it meant to be allergic to pasta?

    Technically, what it means if someone is really “allergic to pasta” is that they suffer actual allergic reaction when they eat pasta. The reality is that they probably aren’t allergic to “pasta” but they are allergic to some ingredient IN the pasta. It could wheat, gluten, eggs or maybe something else depending on how the pasta is made.
